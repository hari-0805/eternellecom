Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Celebrations

At Eternelle, we believe every love story is singular — and every celebration should reflect that uniqueness. We are not merely event planners; we are architects of memory, weavers of atmosphere, and orchestrators of emotion.

From intimate garden weddings to grand ballroom galas, our team of dedicated artisans handles every detail with white-glove precision: breathtaking décor, curated cuisine, and cinematic photography that captures the soul of your moment.

Based in Chennai, serving across India. Every event is a masterpiece in the making.

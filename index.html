Our Story

Crafting Unforgettable Celebrations

At Eternelle, we believe every love story is singular and every celebration should reflect that uniqueness. We are not merely event planners — we are architects of memory, weavers of atmosphere, and orchestrators of emotion.

From intimate garden weddings to grand ballroom galas, our team handles every detail with white-glove precision: breathtaking decor, bridal makeup, sacred garlands, curated cuisine, cinematic photography, and live DJ entertainment.

Based in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu — currently serving all of Tamil Nadu, with plans to expand across India soon.

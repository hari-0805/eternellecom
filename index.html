About Us

We Create Beautiful Weddings

At Eternelle, we believe every love story is singular and every celebration should reflect that uniqueness. We are not merely event planners — we are architects of memory, weavers of atmosphere, and orchestrators of emotion.

From intimate garden weddings to grand ballroom galas, our team handles every detail with white-glove precision: breathtaking decor, bridal makeup, sacred garlands, curated cuisine, cinematic photography, and live DJ entertainment.

Based in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu — currently serving all of Tamil Nadu, with plans to expand across India soon.

Questions

Common Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive.

We recommend booking at least 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (November to February). Popular dates fill up quickly, and early booking gives us maximum time to curate every detail of your celebration perfectly. That said, we do accommodate shorter timelines — reach out and we will always try our best.
Absolutely. We currently serve all of Tamil Nadu and are actively expanding our reach across India. We have coordinated events in Coimbatore, Chennai, Ooty, Madurai, and beyond. For destination weddings outside Tamil Nadu, please contact us early so we can plan travel and logistics with care.
Every package we offer is a starting point, not a rigid structure. You are completely free to add, remove, or swap services based on your needs and budget. Our Premium tier is entirely bespoke by design. We prefer to build your package around your vision rather than ask you to fit into ours.
Our bridal makeup service includes a pre-wedding consultation and trial session, day-of makeup application using premium brands, and touch-up support throughout the event. We offer traditional South Indian bridal looks, modern HD makeup, and fusion styles. Hair styling can be added as an upgrade or is included in the Standard and Premium packages.
Our team is trained to handle the unexpected with calm and grace. We always prepare contingency plans for weather, vendor delays, and timing changes. Your dedicated event coordinator will be present throughout the day to manage everything behind the scenes, so you never have to worry about a single thing on your most important day.
Yes. Our Garlands and Florals services cover everything from the bridal jaimala and groom garlands to mandap decorations, entrance arches, table centerpieces, and full venue floral installations. All garlands are handcrafted fresh on your wedding day using jasmine, marigold, rose, and other seasonal blooms of your choice.
We begin with a complimentary consultation to understand your vision. Once we align on a package and proposal, a 30% advance secures your date. A second installment of 40% is due 30 days before your event, and the final 30% is settled on or after the event day. All payment terms are clearly outlined in our agreement with full transparency.
We warmly welcome you to meet us in person at our studio in Sathyamangalam, Tamil Nadu. We can also arrange virtual consultations via video call for clients who are planning from outside the region. A face-to-face meeting always helps us better understand your vision and build the trust that makes our partnership truly special.
